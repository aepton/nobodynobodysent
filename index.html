Done? Good. Hi! I'm Abe, a news apps developer at the Chicago Tribune (though this isn't a Tribune project, and I'm just writing my perspective as a citizen and news-app developer). I'll be spending Election Night (Tuesday, March 18, 2014) in the newsroom, not working but observing, trying to pass on the most entertaining bits of the chaos on this page. This isn't really a liveblog; it's more like a live-blank-canvas-on-the-Internet. Whatever I can throw together quickly that captures something interesting, I'll put here. Stuff like tweets:

Along with maps, snippets of conversations overheard, updates on vote totals and interesting stats as soon as we get them, the usual assortment of memes, and increasingly-belabored jokes as the night wears on. Won't you join me?
